About Our Fest

Celebrating Innovation, Culture, and Creativity

Epistemico is the annual techno-cultural fest of Mahila Engineering College, Ajmer. It is a legacy of our college that started since the very beginning of this institution. This is the 12th edition of Epistemico. Epistemico, the name itself means celebrating technocrats and feminism, is the biggest extravaganza held under the supervision of the Creative Arts Society. Events are conducted under the four major committees: Technical, Cultural, Innovation, and Literary, with participants from various colleges of Rajasthan and nearby states.

About Our College

Empowering Women, Engineering the Future

At Mahila Engineering College Ajmer, it's about pushing boundaries, sparking innovation, and fostering a vibrant community where ideas turn into reality. As Rajasthan’s first and India’s second all-women engineering college, we are a fusion of brilliance, ambition, and creativity. From national hackathons and cutting-edge research to top industry placements and groundbreaking startups. But Mahila Engineering College is more than academics; it’s a canvas of culture, where art meets algorithms, dance meets design, and ambition meets opportunity. Whether it's the electrifying energy of Epistemico, the pulse of our tech clubs, or the fire of entrepreneurial ventures, every corner of our campus sparks possibility. Here, we don’t wait for the future—we create it.
